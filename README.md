# HablaCaribe Agentic Learning Engine

**HablaCaribe prepares Caribbean people to communicate, connect, and collaborate across languages and cultures.**

This repository documents the proposed adaptive learning engine behind HablaCaribe for the FutureCaribbean Buildathon.

HablaCaribe is not designed as another language-learning app. It is the first digital expression of a Caribbean-centred learning system developed through years of research, implementation, and testing across schools, online learning environments, and organisational development.

## The core idea

Most language tools begin with the question:

> What language do you want to learn?

HablaCaribe begins with a different question:

> What do you want to do?

The learner may want to:

- pass an exam
- study abroad
- communicate with customers
- expand a business
- build regional partnerships

From there, HablaCaribe builds a personalized pathway that develops the language, cultural understanding, communication skills, and confidence needed to achieve that goal.

## Why agentic AI

Our first prototype used generative AI and validated the learner journey. It also revealed a limitation: a chatbot could not make the continuous instructional decisions that great teachers make.

Great teachers know when to encourage, challenge, explain differently, move forward, or slow down.

The Buildathon goal is to build an adaptive learning engine capable of making those instructional decisions in real time.

## Buildathon focus

The goal is not to build the full commercial product in 21 days.

The goal is to build a focused, runnable demonstration of the adaptive learning loop:

1. learner goal
2. safety and suitability check
3. purpose agent
4. dynamic learner profile
5. learning experience engine
6. learning activity
7. assessment agent
8. memory update
9. recommended next experience
