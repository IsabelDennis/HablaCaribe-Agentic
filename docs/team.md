**Role:** Co-Founder, Bilingual Education and Regional Partnerships Lead

Elizabeth Dennis brings more than thirty years of experience as a bilingual educator and has spent her career helping English- and Spanish-speaking people and businesses communicate across the Caribbean through education and trade.

Her expertise informs HablaCaribe's language strategy, bilingual learning design, cultural context, and regional trade use cases.
